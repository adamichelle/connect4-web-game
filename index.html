Connect 4

How to Play
  1. Click on white circle spots to drop checkers into the last empty slot
  2. A win happens when:
    • You get 4 of the same colors in a row horizontally.
    • You get 4 of the same colors in a column vertically.
    • You get 4 of the same colors diagonally from top left to bottom right.
    • You get 4 of the same colors diagonally from bottom right to top left.
